Design notes: the two link columns only pair up on phones (desktop keeps the current 4-column spread). Legal moves to an inline row because those five words are recognisable at a glance — they don't need vertical rhythm. Badge pills wrap in one cluster instead of stacking. If a third link column ever appears (e.g. "AI tools"), the same 2-up grid absorbs it as a second row. Alternative considered and rejected: accordions — they hide links behind taps and hurt SEO crawl of footer links.
