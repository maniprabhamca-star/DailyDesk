isnet-quint8.onnx — background-removal model for DiemDesk's on-device
Remove Background tool.

Derived from the IS-Net "isnet-general-use" pretrained model of the DIS
project (Highly Accurate Dichotomous Image Segmentation):
  https://github.com/xuebinqin/DIS
  Copyright (c) 2022 Xuebin Qin et al.
  Licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0:
  https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0

Modifications: converted to ONNX and dynamically quantized (QUInt8) to
reduce download size. The model runs entirely in the user's browser via
ONNX Runtime Web; images are never uploaded.
