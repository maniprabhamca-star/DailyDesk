## Version A — privacy / on-device angle (recommended, ~380 words)

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**DiemDesk Champions On-Device Document Privacy — No Uploads, No Accounts**

*The privacy-first document toolkit keeps your files on your own device — a growing set of free tools that never send your documents to a server.*

MARIETTA, Ga. — [Publish date] — Most online PDF and document tools work the same way: you hand your file to a company's servers and trust that it's handled well. DiemDesk (diemdesk.com) takes the opposite approach. Its everyday tools run entirely inside your web browser, so your documents never leave your device — there is nothing to upload, and nothing for anyone else to store, scan, or leak.

That single design choice changes what's possible. Because files are processed locally, DiemDesk suits contracts, IDs, medical records, and other sensitive material that shouldn't be uploaded to a stranger's cloud. It keeps working without a connection once a page has loaded, and it skips the wait of sending a large file to a server and back.

The toolkit covers the jobs people reach for every day. You can [convert a PDF to Word](https://diemdesk.com/pdf-to-word), [sign a PDF](https://diemdesk.com/sign-pdf), and merge, split, compress, rotate, and redact documents — plus resize and convert images, generate QR codes, and more. There are 35 tools live today, with more rolling out. Every in-browser tool is free and unlimited, with no ads, no watermarks, and no account required.

"People shouldn't have to upload their private documents just to get simple work done," said [Founder name], founder of DiemDesk. "We built DiemDesk so the work happens on your device — you can open your browser's network tab and watch: nothing is sent anywhere."

DiemDesk is free to use today. A paid Pro plan is on the way — adding on-device batch processing, an encrypted file vault, OCR, and AI document assistants — and early users can lock in founding-member pricing at diemdesk.com/pricing.
